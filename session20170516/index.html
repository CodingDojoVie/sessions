What is a Coding Dojo?


  • Safe place outside of work and away from production code
  • Relax and slow down
  • You don't have to finish
  • Focus on doing it right
  • Deliberate Practice => Learning
  • Learning => Sharing => Learning

Before we start

Form pairs

 

  • Agree on language
  • Have a computer ready

Part 1

Diving Into

Pick one Principle

Work on
your Principle

1/2

  • Discuss the meaning and impact
    • What problem(s) does it address?
  • Make up a super simple code sample that suffers from a violation
    • What (additional) problems can you spot with this violation?
  • Propose a fix for the violation

Work on
your Principle

2/2

  • Describe a situation where adherence helps
  • What are typical signs of violation?
  • Where do you see danger in introducing a violation?
    • While writing new code?
    • During refactoring?

Share your Results
